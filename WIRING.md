## Display, Pin, Cable color

VCC,VDD (3.3V)            - Green
GND,GND                   - Brown
SCL (Clock),P0.27         - Blue
SDA (MOSI),P0.26          - Yellow
RES (Reset),P0.05         - Orange
DC (Data/Cmd),P0.06       - Red
CS (Chip Select),P0.08    - Black
BLK (Backlight),P0.07     - White
